UX Friction Points

Where users drop off — opportunities to improve

  • 1. Hard gate at 2 analyses: Anonymous trial never resets. Permanently blocked after 2 uses. No "come back next month" option.
  • 2. No free signup path at gate: Upgrade modal only shows $40 and $100 options. No way to create a free account with 2/month resets.
  • 3. Three redirects to payment: Results → login page → workspace modal → Stripe checkout. Each transition is a drop-off risk.
  • 4. Email capture is passive: Form sits below results — user may never scroll to it. No modal, timed trigger, or exit-intent.
  • 5. Free vs anonymous confusion: Anonymous = 2 lifetime. Free authenticated = 2/month. No messaging explains the benefit of signing up free.
  • 6. No trial period: No 7-day free Pro trial. User goes from $0 straight to $40/month with no middle ground.
  • 7. Downloads without login: Excel + brief download free without creating account. Reduces motivation to sign up.
